Key Market Insights
🤖
AI is augmenting, not replacing
76% of developers who use AI tools report higher productivity. The fear of AI replacement is dropping among senior engineers who understand how to leverage it strategically.
📈
Data roles see highest demand growth
Data engineering and analytics engineering are the fastest-growing roles in 2024, driven by the explosion of AI infrastructure needs and real-time data pipelines.
☁️
Cloud-native skills command premium
Engineers with AWS, GCP, or Azure certifications earn 22-35% more than counterparts without cloud expertise. Cloud infrastructure engineers rank 3rd in global salary rankings.
🐍
Python dominance continues in 2024
Python remains the #1 language for data professionals for the 4th consecutive year. Combined with SQL proficiency, it's the baseline expectation for virtually all analytical roles.
